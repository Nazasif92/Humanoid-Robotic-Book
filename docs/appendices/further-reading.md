# Appendix D: Further Reading

This appendix provides additional resources for continuing your learning journey in humanoid robotics.

## Official Documentation

- [ROS 2 Documentation](https://docs.ros.org/en/humble/)
- [Gazebo Documentation](https://gazebosim.org/docs)
- [Unity Robotics Hub](https://unity.com/solutions/industrial-robotics)
- [NVIDIA Isaac Documentation](https://docs.nvidia.com/isaac/)
- [Docusaurus Documentation](https://docusaurus.io/docs)

## Recommended Books

- "Programming Robots with ROS" by Morgan Quigley et al.
- "Robotics, Vision and Control" by Peter Corke
- "Probabilistic Robotics" by Sebastian Thrun et al.
- "Springer Handbook of Robotics" edited by Siciliano and Khatib

## Online Courses

- Coursera: "Robotics Specialization" by University of Pennsylvania
- edX: "Introduction to Robotics" by MIT
- Udacity: "Robotics Nanodegree"

## Research Papers

- "ROS: an open-source Robot Operating System" by Quigley et al.
- "The Open Motion Planning Library" by Svestka and Overmars
- "A Survey of Control Architectures for Legged Robots" by Semini et al.

## Communities

- ROS Discourse: https://discourse.ros.org/
- ROS Answers: https://answers.ros.org/
- Gazebo Community: https://community.gazebosim.org/
- Robotics Stack Exchange: https://robotics.stackexchange.com/

## Conferences and Journals

- IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation (ICRA)
- IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS)
- Journal of Field Robotics
- Autonomous Robots Journal
- IEEE Robotics & Automation Magazine
